Welcome to Veda Ankuram

Veda Ankuram is a not-for-profit organization committed to transforming the lives of underprivileged girls in India through education, skill development, and exposure to real-world career opportunities.

We also promote career readiness for all students by organizing free counselling sessions with national and international universities across India, helping them make informed decisions about their future.

Our Mission:

  • To empower underprivileged girls through education and to inspire students across India by connecting them with higher education opportunities and future career pathways.

Our Vision:

  • A future where every child, regardless of their background, can access world-class education and grow into confident, capable contributors to society.

What we do:

  • Provide access to quality education and life skills to girls from underserved communities.
  • Organize career counselling visits for school students to top universities across India.
  • Enable students to interact with university faculty, counselors, and current students.
  • Provide insights on courses, scholarships, fee structures, eligibility, and credit transfer options.
  • Run a podcast series covering dynamic and futuristic topics for youth and educators.
  • Support girl empowerment initiatives through CSR collaborations, enabling companies to contribute meaningfully to education and social welfare.

We actively collaborate with corporates and institutions through their Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) programs. These partnerships fund the education, training, and personal development of underprivileged girls, enabling them to access quality resources, mentorship, and real-world learning opportunities.

    Helping Students Make Informed Choices for Their Future

    The National Mobility Program is an initiative by Veda Ankuram designed to empower students with the information and exposure they need to make confident, well-informed decisions about their higher education. Over six days, students travel across key cities and visit some of India’s top private universities, including Ashoka, Jindal, Shiv Nadar, Shoolini, Atlas, and Woxsen.

    The core purpose of this program is to introduce students to a variety of universities and the wide range of courses they offer. During campus visits, students interact directly with university faculty, counselors, and even current students to understand the academic environment, teaching methodology, campus life, and infrastructure. They receive valuable information about scholarship opportunities, fee structures, credit transfers, eligibility criteria, and admission processes.

    By experiencing these campuses firsthand and engaging in meaningful conversations, students can evaluate which university and course aligns best with their aspirations. This program is not just a tour—it's a life-shaping opportunity to ask the right questions, explore the right paths, and build the right future.
